TaskMate Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 27, 2025

Audio Recording Permission

TaskMate uses the RECORD_AUDIO permission exclusively for voice-to-text functionality to help you create notes and tasks quickly.

  • Data Collection: Audio is processed locally on your device only
  • Storage: No audio recordings are permanently stored
  • Sharing: Audio data is never shared with third parties
  • Purpose: Converting speech to text for productivity features only

Data Security

All voice processing happens on-device using Android's built-in speech recognition. No audio leaves your device.

Your Rights

You can disable voice features at any time in the app settings without affecting other functionality.
